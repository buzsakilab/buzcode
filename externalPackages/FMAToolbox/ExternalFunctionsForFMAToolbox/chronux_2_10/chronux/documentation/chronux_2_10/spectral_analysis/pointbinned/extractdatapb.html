extractdatapb

PURPOSE

DESCRIPTION 

 Extract segements of binned point process data between t(1) and t(2)
 Usage: data=extractdatapb(data,Fs,t)

 Input:

 vector

 Note that sampling frequency and t have to be in consistent units

 data: data between t(1) and t(2)
